<h2>What This Policy Covers</h2>

<p>This policy describes how Old Skool Board collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app through the Atlassian Marketplace on Jira Cloud.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Data We Access</h2>

<p>Old Skool Board accesses the following data from your Jira Cloud instance via the Jira REST API:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Board and project configuration</strong> — board names, columns, workflows</li>
  <li><strong>Issue data</strong> — issue summaries, statuses, issue types, assignees, priorities</li>
  <li><strong>User information</strong> — display names and account identifiers (as provided by Jira for display purposes)</li>
</ul>

<p>This data is read live from Jira each time you use the app. It is not copied to external systems.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Data We Store</h2>

<p>Old Skool Board uses the <a href="https://developer.atlassian.com/platform/forge/runtime-reference/storage-api/">Forge Storage API</a> to store a small amount of configuration data:</p>

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>What</th><th>Purpose</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Selected board preference</td><td>Remembers which board you last viewed in a project</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Board display preferences</td><td>Your display settings (e.g. completed item retention period)</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Story ordering</td><td>Custom ordering of items on the board</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

<p>These preferences are scoped per user using your Atlassian account identifier. No display names, email addresses, or other profile data is stored.</p>

<p>All stored data resides entirely within Atlassian's cloud infrastructure and is encrypted at rest by Atlassian.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Data We Do Not Collect</h2>

<p>Old Skool Board does <strong>not</strong>:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Use analytics or tracking services</li>
  <li>Set cookies</li>
  <li>Send data to external servers or third parties</li>
  <li>Collect email addresses, IP addresses, or device information</li>
  <li>Log user activity</li>
  <li>Use advertising or profiling</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Where Data Is Stored</h2>

<p>All data remains within Atlassian's cloud infrastructure. The app runs on the <a href="https://developer.atlassian.com/platform/forge/">Atlassian Forge</a> platform. There are no external servers, databases, or third-party services.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Third-Party Sharing</h2>

<p>We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Data Retention and Deletion</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Stored preferences are retained for as long as the app is installed</li>
  <li>When the app is uninstalled, all Forge Storage data associated with the installation is deleted</li>
  <li>There is no data retained after uninstallation</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Your Rights</h2>

<h3>Under GDPR (European Economic Area)</h3>

<p>If you are in the EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact <a href="mailto:support@spindriftlabs.net">support@spindriftlabs.net</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Legal basis for processing:</strong> We process account identifiers on the basis of legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) — specifically, to provide per-user preferences within the app.</p>

<h3>Under CCPA (California)</h3>

<p>We do not sell or share personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act.</p>

<hr>

<h2>International Data Transfers</h2>

<p>Data is handled within Atlassian's cloud infrastructure. Atlassian's own data transfer mechanisms (including EU-US Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses) apply to any transfers between regions.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Children's Privacy</h2>

<p>Old Skool Board is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.</p>
