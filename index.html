What it does differently

Boards reward movement. We wanted one that rewards finishing.

[PARENT-VISIBLE]

The story stays in view.

Parent items pin to the left of their swimlane while sub-tasks move through To Do, In Progress, and Done. You always see what's being finished — not just what's moving.

[FULL-BOARD]

Built for the whole board, at once.

Tuned for desktop standups. A typical sprint fits without scrolling. No collapsing, no hover-to-reveal — what you see is the whole board.

[JIRA-NATIVE]

Lives inside Jira Cloud.

Runs on Atlassian Forge. Reads live from the Jira REST API — nothing copied out, no cookies, no analytics, no external servers.
